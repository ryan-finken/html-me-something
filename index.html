Ryan and Corey's Cat Family

Corey and I met in 2013. Since then, we've moved in and introduced our cats, added two more, and gotten married! Get to know the whole gang down below.

Hugo | Leia | Loretta | Miles | Zoe |

Zoe

Zoe is the first cat I adopted. I found her when she was about 6 months old, and have had her since 2010. She has a bit of a mean streak, but she can be quite charming once you get to know her. She's the oldest cat in the house.

Loretta

My wife adopted Loretta before I met either of them; they have been together since 2011, and Corey adopted Loretta when she was a very young kitten. Loretta hated me for the first 2 years we lived together, but we're buds now. The key to getting along with Loretta is to respect her boundaries.

Hugo

Hugo is a sweet boy who loves to cuddle and laze about. He is very fond of Corey, and everyone else in the house is fond of him. Corey adopted him before we met, and he is a little older than Loretta.He is probably the biggest cat you've ever seen.

Miles

Miles is the first cat Corey and I adopted together. He is very friendly, funny, and chill; he can be a jerk to his sisters sometimes, but really loves his brother Hugo. Miles is about 4 years old.

Leia

Leia is a squirrelly mini panther born on May 4th, 2017. When she is not chasing ghosts only visible to her, she likes to sleep with Corey or me, and doesn't particularly care if we want her or not.
